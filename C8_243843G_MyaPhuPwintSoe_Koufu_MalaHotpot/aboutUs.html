We believe that delicious and nutritious food should be an essential part of every student's day. We're excited to bring the vibrant flavors of Mala to the NYP canteen, offering a customizable and exciting meal option that will keep you energized and focused.

We understand that students have diverse tastes and dietary needs, so we've created a build-your-own-bowl concept that puts you in charge. Choose from a variety of fresh vegetables, lean proteins, and different types of noodles. Whether you're a vegetarian, a meat-lover, or have specific dietary restrictions, you can create a Mala bowl that's prefect for you.

Our signature Mala sauce and soup is made with a blend of aromatic spices and Sichuan peppercorns, providing a delicious kick without being overly spicy (we can adjust the spice level to your preference!). We're committed to using high-quality ingredients and preparing everything fresh daily, ensuring a tasty and wholesome meal.

At NYP Koufu Mala Hotpot, we're passionate about providing a quick, affordable, and satisfying meal option for students. We know you're busy with classes and activities, so we strive to serve you efficiently without compromsing on quality of flavor.

Come visit us at the NYP Koufu and discover your new favourite lunch spot! We're confident you'll love the taste and the flexibility of our Mala bowls.
